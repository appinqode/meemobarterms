Meemobar

Terms of Use

1. Introduction

Meemobar is a lightweight menu bar clipboard manager for macOS that automatically saves everything you copy and lets you quickly search, reuse and organize your clipboard history. Perfect for developers, writers, designers and anyone who copies and pastes throughout the day.
